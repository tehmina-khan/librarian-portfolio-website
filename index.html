WHAT I CAN DO FOR YOUR LIBRARY!

01

Record Maintenance

Maintain accurate records of all library materials in Excel and manual registers for precise cataloging and easy tracking.

02

Acquisition of Materials

Source books, journals, and digital resources that meet user needs, securing the best value through trusted vendors and competitive quotations, locally and internationally.

03

Cataloging and Classification

Label, catalog, and classify materials using recognized systems such as Dewey Decimal and Library of Congress, ensuring a well-structured and easily navigable collection.

04

Shelving and Arrangement

Arrange books and resources systematically to maintain order, improve accessibility, and prevent loss or misplacement.

05

Digital Resource Access

Integrate digital solutions to provide access to e-books, online journals, databases, and digital libraries, supporting both in-person and remote learning and research.

06

Stationery & Supplies Management

Organize and manage library stationery such as accession stamps, book pockets, book cards, accession registers, and issue registers to ensure smooth daily operations.
